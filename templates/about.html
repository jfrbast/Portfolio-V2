What?


There are two parts in this portfolio:

1. The Game

A point and click game where you have to walk around to find the houses that match the projects.

2. The "basic" portfolio

More basic, with a direct list of projects without going through the game.

Why?


I wanted to do something different, something that reflects who I am.

I like video games, the style is based on games like Stardew Valley.


I like to make a lot of projects, it makes me happy :)

How to play?


A point and click game is a game like League of Legends where your character moves according to your clicks.


You need to move on the map to find the houses according to projects to have access to the corresponding page.


No worries, it's not rocket science.
